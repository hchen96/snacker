FAQS

Frequently Ask Question

Can I search for a snack?

Yes! Go to the home page, type your favourite snack name in the search bar and hit enter!

Can I review a snack?

Yes! In the individual page of a snack, click on write a review if you have not already written one for this snack!

What does the admin eat?

Whatever he can find next to his keyboard.

How to create an account?

Go to the registration page, by clicking the "Register" button from the navigation bar (top-right corner) and complete all the fields.

I am a company representative. How can I register?

In the registration page, fill in your official company's name and use an email address belonging to your company's official domain to register.
